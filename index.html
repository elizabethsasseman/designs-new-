B I O

I am a designer and entrepreneur based out of the foothills of the Colorado Front Range. I grew up in Indiana where I was always encouraged to explore my interests. Growing up I developed a lifelong love of the outdoors, art, and science. Today, I apply design to these disciplines to create new and simple ways of finding understanding and meaning. I am particularly interested in how people use and interact with objects and spaces. This has led me to consider how the principles of user experience (UX) design might be applied to areas outside of technology. I am especially interested in architecture and our experience with interior spaces.

C U R R E N T L Y

Fine-tuning my unique idea of creating meaningful spaces in people's homes by pairing photography with interiors.

B U S I N E S S | V A L U E S

Natural

We have an enormous environmental crisis happening in the world right now. I believe that if we can apply nature to the world we create, the products we create, and the ideas we create, we might survive.

Simple

I believe that deep understanding, brilliant ideas, and healthy lifestyles are developed by creating and capturing simplicity.

Inventive

It is my priority to push boundaries and the limits of existing best practices. My goal is to create exceptional designs, and I don't believe that will happen by remaining in line. I value hand-crafted experiences, built from new ideas and systems.

Usable

I believe the most important aspect of design is it's potential to improve an experience. My goal with design is to create something that is intuitive, easy-to-use, and natural to interact with; whether that be an app, a room in a house, or a physical product.

Adventurous

I have an entrepreneurial spirit and love to wear many hats. If something isn't working, I explore and iterate until I find something that works perfectly. I believe a great business is one that loves to wander far and wide in order to learn more and build more.

Modern

In order to design for today and tomorrow, I research my market of users and customers and analyze my findings in order to develop something that aligns with their evolving needs.
