Philosophy Prints Materials
I have developed a new and unique way of bringing photography into people's homes. By analyzing a photo's components and the feeling it evokes, I carefully consider what the photo might bring to a room. I use contrast and the elements of interior design to pair my photos with rooms to enhance their natural aesthetics. This unique idea brings design into the world of photography. Rather than passive artistic creation, I strive to use thoughtful design to create a connection between a photo, a room, and the people in it.
My photography is a reflection of the simplicity of nature, and a reminder of how fragile the environment around us is. My photos are meant to create a connection between the wonderful outdoors and our homes by bringing a piece of it inside. In order for all of us to give back together, I donate 1% of the profits from every sale to environmental nonprofits.
Each of my photos is designed to fill a space in a particular and thoughtful way. My standards are that of an interior designer, and my goal is to make sure the placement of the artwork in your home has been carefully considered.

P R I N T S

Bamboo Print

This paper option is made of 90% bamboo fibers and 10% cotton. The natural white tones in this paper enhance depth and richness in vibrant photos. Bamboo really highlights the sensuality of images, and is an environmentally-friendly option. This option requires additional framing.

Acrylic Print

Acrylic prints are the most luxurious option and have the highest quality. They have a modern feel, can appear to be illuminated when properly lit, and explode with color. Sharp details and vibrant colors are enhanced with this elegant option. Images are first printed on FujiFlex with a LightJet printer, and then mounted onto an 1/8" black sintra board and flame-polished OP3 acrylic with pvc backing.

Metal Print

Metal prints give off reflective qualities, and are exceptionally detailed and clear. ChromaLuxe prints are lightweight yet still incredibly durable. They are fade-, water-, and scratch-resistant making the transporation of them easy. Images are first printed onto a ChromaLuxe glossy aluminum panel and then mounted.
