D E S I G N S

I design and develop products that satisfy user needs. My long-term goal is to source entirely environmentally-friendly materials in order to offer positive alternatives to existing products, and to set a high standard in developing new products. My vison is to create a business that not only positively impacts the environment, but also motivates people to lead simple and sustainable lifestyles.
Each of my product's logos have been designed with simplicity in mind to reflect the simplicity we often find in nature. With underlying environmental values behind my business as a whole, every brand's logo has a similar modern and simplistic design. This collection of brands is a reflection of our environment, and is meant to inspire a new care for the products we consume and the effects they have on us and the world we live in.
