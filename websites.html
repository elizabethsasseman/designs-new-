B I O

I have experience working as a UI/UX Designer, a Front-End Developer, and a Graphic/Visual Designer for several companies, both small and large. I set out to pursue user experience design and haven't looked back. My interest in design doesn't end with technology - I am equally interested in considering how UX can be applied to interior design, architecture, physical product design, and in the art of cooking.

G E N E R A L | A P P R O A C H

Competitive Analysis

My goal is to figure out what your competition has to offer and how your products or services compare. I then use this information to determine how your website could be improved to better present your business, to tell your story, and to strategize ways to create a competitive advantage.

User Research

I do research in order to better understand your users. From there I am able to develop personas based on the understanding I've gathered. And once I understand the people who use your technology in a more personal and relatable way, I'm able to move toward developing ideas and designs that are testable.

Brainstorming & Sketching

This phase is where I begin to bridge the gap between your existing website, what our user research findings are telling us, and what your business goals and strategies are.

Information Architecture

Usability is just as dependent on the organization and design of content, as it is dependent on the functionality and visual design of the whole website. I craft and organize content based on what your users want and what will help your users connect better with your business and its values.

Brand & Voice

My goal is to strengthen and develop your brand and your voice. I help create color themes and style guidelines that your business can confidentally follow. And I help develop a voice that sympathizes with, excites, and is relatable to your users.

Prototyping

I create prototypes that are ready for testing and development. Whether it be a pixel-perfect mockup, or a user flow that describes the new architecture of your site, my goal at the end of the day is to find a solution to the problem and make it ready for testing.

User Testing

I test solutions with real users. During testing, I analyze user behaviors and interactions with the solution. My goal is to gain insights and understanding into why a user interacts the way he or she does, and then learn from and iterate on the solution accordingly. Once iterated upon and thoroughly tested, my designs are ready for development!

Development

In addition to the design process, I provide some front-end development services in order to make these designs a reality. I am able to build websites from scratch or edit existing websites. Your website may then be handed off to a back-end developer if necessary.
